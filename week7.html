Iot Infrastructure

Reflection:

Infrastructure Setup:


- Intelligent irrigation tracking system
- Ability to follow the needs (is it necessary to prune, is the grass growing, is there any disease in the plants, vitamin value of the soil, etc.)
- Intelligent temperature adjustment system
- Weekly sunshine hours distribution report or instant display
- Electricity source based on solar panel

Constraints:


- Possibility of constant optimization of camera angles depending on the plants to be grown in them
- Possibility of damage to the software that may be caused by errors that were not noticed in a timely manner
- Damages that may occur in physical layers
- Possibility of expensive equipment to provide this system
- At the same time, the possibility that the software that can control this system is expensive
